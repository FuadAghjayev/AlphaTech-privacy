This Privacy Policy explains how AlphaTech Plotter (the "App") handles information. The App is developed and provided by AlphaTech.

1. App Purpose

AlphaTech Plotter helps users access a large collection of mobile device screen dimensions across many phone brands and models, and export those layouts to a Bluetooth thermal printer for planning cutting operations. The App runs primarily on-device and is designed for offline usage.

2. Permissions We Request

The App requests the following permissions to provide core functionality:

2.1 Bluetooth Permissions

The App requires Bluetooth permissions (BLUETOOTH_SCAN and BLUETOOTH_CONNECT) to:

  • Discover nearby Bluetooth thermal printers
  • Connect to and communicate with Bluetooth printers
  • Send print jobs containing layout dimensions

Important: We do not use Bluetooth to track your location or collect any personal data.

2.2 Location Permission

The App requests location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) solely to comply with Android's system requirement for Bluetooth scanning on Android 12 and above. This is a mandatory Android OS requirement and we do NOT collect, store, transmit, or use any location data. Your location is never accessed or tracked by our App.

2.3 Internet Permission

Internet permission is included in the manifest but is not actively used for data collection. It may be used for future updates or optional features.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not request, collect, or store personal data from users. Specifically, the following items are not collected by the App:

  • Full name or personal identifiers
  • Email addresses (unless provided via user-initiated contact outside the App)
  • Location data (GPS, network location, or any geolocation information)
  • Photos, media, or files from your device
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, or similar)
  • Bluetooth device information beyond connection purposes
  • Print history or content

4. Printing & Bluetooth Communication

AlphaTech Plotter connects to Bluetooth thermal printers to send layout data for printing. All communication happens locally between your device and the printer:

  • Printing is initiated explicitly by the user
  • Print data is transmitted directly to the connected Bluetooth printer
  • No print content is stored, logged, or transmitted to external servers
  • Bluetooth connections are established only when you choose to print

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not transmit user data to third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Because it is designed for on-device use, internet connectivity is not required for core features. If you choose to contact the developer via email, that communication occurs through your email provider and is outside the scope of this policy.

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store personal information, there is no user data stored by AlphaTech Plotter on our servers. Any temporary data used for rendering layouts or preparing print jobs remains on your device and is cleared when the App or the print workflow ends.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Control

You have full control over the permissions granted to the App:

  • You can revoke Bluetooth or Location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Revoking Bluetooth permissions will prevent the App from connecting to printers
  • Revoking Location permission will prevent Bluetooth scanning on Android 12+
  • No data is transmitted or stored even when permissions are granted

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
